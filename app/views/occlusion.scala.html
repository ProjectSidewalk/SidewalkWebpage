Occlusion

Occlusion is when you can't see something at all. In these cases, you should place an Occlusion label. This should rarely be used, so only place the Occlusion label when a sidewalk, ramp, or other accessibility problem cannot be viewed from any angle due to obstructions, such as cars.

Another rare case of occlusion is when a long stretch of sidewalk is totally blocked from view by a short wall between the road and sidewalk. Label this using the same technique as for extended missing sidewalks.
