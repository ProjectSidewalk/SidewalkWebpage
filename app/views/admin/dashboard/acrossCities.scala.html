Engagement funnels #

Of the people who arrive, how many make it through each step, for each city. Two funnels: the mapping funnel follows the Explore onboarding (tutorial → first label → first mission), and the contribution funnel is the broader “did they contribute anything (labeling or validation) and finish a mission”. Bars are normalized to visitors (= 100%); the percentage on each step is its share of the step before it (the drop-off).

Two caveats. Anonymous visitors are counted per browser (a new id each time cookies are cleared), so the top of each funnel counts sessions, not unique people, and headline conversion reads low. The mapping tool is desktop-only — mobile visitors are redirected to a landing page and can't proceed, so in the “Desktop vs mobile” view mobile drops off early by design. In the mapping funnel, “took a step” means a user walked some distance in a real (non-tutorial) mission.
