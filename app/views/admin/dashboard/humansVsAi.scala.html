Humans vs AI #

How does the AI compare to people on this deployment? Project Sidewalk's AI plays three distinct roles, and this page compares each against human contributions: it places labels (the auto-labeler), it validates human labels (agree / disagree), and it tags labels. AI isn't yet at human level, so the comparisons keep the two clearly separate rather than blending them.

Per-validator-type agreement lives on Data Quality, and per-person ranking on Contributors. This page is strictly the human-vs-AI cut.
